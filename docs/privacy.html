Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2022

The "Keyboard & Mouse for Xbox xCloud" browser extension ("We") do not store, send, or otherwise access any data about you.

We don't even use an anlytics provider inside the extension, or have an application server to send data to.

The only network connections the "Keyboard & Mouse for Xbox xCloud" browser extension makes are the built-in browser's "sync" storage service (which is only used to synchronize your saved settings between your browsers), as well as ExtensionPay, a third party payment provider (privacy policy visible on the "FAQ" section of https://extensionpay.com).

The documentation website, accessible at https://idolize.github.io/xcloud-keyboard-mouse/, does use a very basic analytics provider (Goatcounter) to track pageviews - but no personal data is tracked or stored. The Goatcounter Privacy Policy can be viewed at https://www.goatcounter.com/help/privacy

However, We may link third-party service providers or other websites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

  • Google Forms (linked to as an optional survey you can complete to submit feedback)

    Their Privacy Policy can be viewed at https://policies.google.com/privacy

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies or practices of any third party sites or services.

We may update Our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify You of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

We will let you know via email and/or a prominent notice on Our Service, prior to the change becoming effective and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
